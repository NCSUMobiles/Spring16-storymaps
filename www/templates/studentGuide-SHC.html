Submitting Immunization Documentations

You must submit records of required immunizations online at https://healthweb.ncsu.edu but you are also required to submit official copies of your immunization documentation (in English) in person, via fax or through mail to Student Health Services.

Mail:
Student Health Services
Attention: Immunization Department
2815 Cates Avenue
Raleigh, NC 27695-7304

Fax:1-877-221-6278

Check your secure messages at healthweb.ncsu.edu for updates and communication from the Immunization Office. For immunization-related questions, please email immunizations@ncsu.edu. All email communication should include your full name and NCSU student ID #. You can find more information in the following site: http://healthcenter.ncsu.edu/about-us/our-policies/immunization-compliance.

Appointments are required. To schedule an appointment call 919-515-7107 or book your appointment online through HealthWeb.
