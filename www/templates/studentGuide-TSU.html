Getting Your Student Id Card


It all starts with your campus ID card, which can be used to access a variety of services on campus, including our AllCampus debit spending account.

With your card you can :

Make on-campus purchases through the AllCampus debit account
Access your meal plan
Enter your residence hall and other buildings with restricted access
Work out at the gym
Check out a book at the library
Access your residence hall laundry account
